Bridlington Street Food

Yorkshire's hub of global foods..

The Birth

Bridlington Street Food Club was launched in December 2019 in Bridlington's Promenade after multiple successful outdoor events in 2018. Our aims are to bring you the very best in global street food all year round from a rotating roster of local traders.

BSF Singapore chilli crab

Origins

Jung Born in Pohang, South Korea moved to the UK in 1994, it was here he met Joanna. A Polish born chef who had moved to the UK just two years before Jung. The two shared a passion for world foods, in which Bridlington Street Foods was born.

Jung's old street, South Korea

Our Cuisines

Made to order, served fresh.

UK

Street food from the North, The South and the Midlands. Specialising in Yorkshire foods.

USA

Focusing on American Diner food, from breakfast waffles to succulent burgers and fries.

Jamaica

From the crease to the edge. Take your taste buds on a journey to central Kingston.

China

From quirky starters to contemporary main dishes, come taste our traditional Chinese street grub.

Japan

Sushi, Udon, Tofu and more. We pride ourselves in our Asian food pallet.

Thailand

Test your taste against Thailands four main seasonings: salty, sweet, sour, and spicy.
