Amenities


Music

Live music throughout the week. Bands, DJ's and karoke! We also have a free to use retro jukebox. Come down and show us your best song.

Food

Feeling peckish? Fast food served every day of the week from open till close. Dine in or outside in our heated outdoor dining shelter.

Games

A range of games machines and consoles. From House Of The Dead shoot em ups to FIFA20 and then all the way back to Mrs Pacman.

Free WiFi

Free high speed WiFi for all our customers. The code is changed daily, so just ask whoever is pulling your pint for the code!

Comedy

Stand up comedy evnets hosted regularly, mostly weeekends. Please see in store for schedule and further details. Free craft ale with every ticket.

Parking

Free parking for all our customers. May we politly remind you not to drink and drive. To provent this our staff can book you a taxi, just ask.
